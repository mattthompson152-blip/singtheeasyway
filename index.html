About Your Teacher

Meet Matt Thompson

I'm a celebrity vocal coach with over 25 years of experience working with some of the biggest names in the entertainment industry. My client list includes recording artists, film stars, TV personalities, and well-known actors and singers from the Bollywood industry.

While I've had the privilege of coaching celebrities, I believe everyone deserves access to professional vocal training. That's why I teach students of all abilities - from complete beginners to seasoned performers.

I'm currently teaching exclusively online from my mobile studioas I travel in my motorhome. Don't worry - I have excellent internet signal and professional recording equipment, so the quality of your lessons will be just as good as in-person training!
